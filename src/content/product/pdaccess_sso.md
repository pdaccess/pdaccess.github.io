## PRODUCTS

PDAccess SSO, helps companies to authenticate their enterprise applications by using central authentication logic.

- PDAccess authenticates your enterprise applications.
- Oauth2, SAML protocols are supported.
- LDAPS is supported
- PDAccess have the ability to proxy all authentications to your Active directory.
- Well known frameworks (java .net vs) are supported.

### Supported Protocols

LDAP/LDAPS, SAML, OAUTH2,
